Watch Tower

250

Watchtowers allow players to survey areas around them, acting as a long-range Odradek, locating Cargo, Cryptobiotes, Chiral crystals, BTs and structures.

The camera mounted at the top of a watchtower can be raised, lowered, zoomed, and can be used to place markers and plan routes.
