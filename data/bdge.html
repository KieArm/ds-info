Bridge

1000

The Bridge is a structure type that is used to cross ravines, rivers, or other hazardous terrains. Bridges have two terminals on both ends that can be liked and can be either 50m or 80m in length.

Notes

Bridge length does not alter material cost or bandwidth used.

Unlocked after connecting the Distribution Center West of Capital Knot City to the Chiral Network.
