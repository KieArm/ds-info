Timefall Shelter

750

Shelters the player from the effects of Timefall and can be interacted with to pass time for 10 minutes in-game. The weather can be checked via the cuff links to see how long to wait out the storm.

The shelter also features a built-in container repair spray to repair any damaged cargo.
